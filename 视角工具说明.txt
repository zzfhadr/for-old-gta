With this script you can manage the virtual camera in the game, thus making good screenshots smile

So, to activate the free movement, hold down the F10
Use the mouse to the camera orientation.
Home -forward//游戏中换视角快捷键
End -Back //
Delete -Left
Page Down -right
Page Up -Up
Insert -Down//酷狗音乐播放暂停快捷键
F12 -accelerated traffic cameras//与显示\关闭交互界面
冲突
Tab -photograph
Backspace -teleport to the camera
