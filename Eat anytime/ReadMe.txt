This mod let's you eat anytime and anywhere by pressing the 'B'. Eating takes
about 4 seconds so be sure to not eat while in a fight. Eat gives you 100
health that means full health by default healthbar.

- How to install
Copy the eat_food.cs into your CLEO folder.
