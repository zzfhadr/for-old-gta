1.不在车内时按V（视角）开启
2.开启后按V关闭
3.开启后长按V顺移至摄像机处并关闭
4.开启后滚轮可调整速度

1. Press V(camera) when not in vehicles to enable it;
2. Press V(camera) when it's enabled to disable it;
3. Holding V(camera) when enabled will put you at the position of the camera and disable it;
4. Scrolling the mouse wheel(prev weapon, next weapon) can change the speed.
